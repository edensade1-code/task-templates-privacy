Privacy Policy

Task Templates for GoHighLevel Chrome Extension

Last updated: December 26, 2024

Summary: We don't collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data. Your templates are stored locally in your browser.

Introduction

Task Templates for GoHighLevel ("the Extension") is developed by ASAP Cash Home Buyers. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Chrome extension.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. The Extension operates entirely within your browser and does not transmit any data to external servers.

Data Storage

The Extension stores the following data locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API:

  • Task Templates: The templates you create (names, titles, descriptions, due date settings)
  • License Key: If you purchase a license, the key is stored locally to validate your purchase
  • Settings: Your extension preferences

This data is stored using chrome.storage.sync, which means it may sync across your Chrome browsers if you're signed into Chrome. This data never leaves the Chrome ecosystem and is not accessible to us.

Data Access

The Extension accesses the following data from GoHighLevel pages:

  • Contact Information: When you apply a template, the Extension reads contact data displayed on the current page (name, phone, address, etc.) to fill in template placeholders

This data is processed entirely within your browser and is never transmitted externally.

Third-Party Services

The Extension does not integrate with any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

If you purchase a license, the payment is processed by LemonSqueezy. Please refer to their privacy policy for information on how they handle payment data.

Permissions

The Extension requires the following Chrome permissions:

  • storage: To save your templates and settings locally
  • activeTab: To interact with the GoHighLevel page when you use templates
  • Host permissions for GoHighLevel domains: To inject the template functionality into GoHighLevel pages

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally in your browser, security is managed by Chrome's built-in security features. We recommend:

  • Keeping your Chrome browser updated
  • Using a strong password for your Google account if using Chrome sync
  • Not sharing your computer with untrusted individuals

Children's Privacy

The Extension is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect your data, there is no personal data for us to provide, modify, or delete. You can clear all Extension data by:

  • Uninstalling the Extension
  • Clearing Chrome's extension storage in browser settings
